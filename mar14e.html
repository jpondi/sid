Streams in the Desert - March 14 🌄

"The people kept their distance, but Moses drew near the thick darkness where God was." (Exodus 20:21) 🌑

God has hidden secrets that even the wise and clever cannot understand. 🙏 Don’t be afraid of these mysteries. Be okay with not knowing everything. Wait patiently. Soon, God will show you the treasures hidden in the darkness—the beautiful and glorious things you can’t see yet. 🌟 The mystery is just a veil covering God’s face. 👁️

Don’t be scared to step into the dark cloud that’s coming over your life. 🌧️ God is in that cloud. On the other side, there is brightness and glory. ✨ “Don’t be surprised by the hard times you’re going through. It’s not strange. Instead, be happy because you’re sharing in Christ’s sufferings.” 🙌 When you feel alone and forgotten, God is close. He’s in the dark cloud. Step into the darkness without fear. Under that covering, you’ll find God waiting for you. 🕊️
