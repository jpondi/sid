Streams in the Desert - March 27 🌵✨

"I don't consider the sufferings of our present life worth comparing with the glory that will be revealed to us" 🙌 (Romans 8:18).

A remarkable thing happened at a wedding in England recently. 💒 A rich young man from an important family - who had been blind since age 10 but still achieved great things - was marrying a beautiful bride he had never seen. 👰‍♂️ Right before the wedding, he underwent special eye treatments. 👁️‍🗨️

The big day arrived with many important guests: 🎩 government leaders, military officers, church leaders, and scholars. The groom, still with bandaged eyes, went to the church. His doctor met them there. 🏥

The bride walked in nervously with her elderly father. 👨‍👧 Would her love finally see the face he only knew by touch? ✋❤️

As she reached the front, she saw something amazing: 😲 The doctor was removing the last bandage from her groom's eyes! 👁️

The groom stepped forward unsteadily, like someone in a dream. 👣 Colorful light fell on his face - but did he see it? 🌈 YES! In that moment, his face showed dignity and joy like never before as he walked to his bride. 🥹

"At last!" she whispered. "At last!" he replied solemnly. 💞

This powerful moment gives us just a small idea of what Heaven will be like! ✨ When we who have struggled through life's hardships finally see Jesus face to face. ✝️
