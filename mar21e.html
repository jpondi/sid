Streams in the Desert - March 21 🌿🌞

According to your faith be it unto you (Matthew 9:29). 🙏✨

"Praying through" means praying until you fully believe, and while you are still praying, you become sure that God has heard you. You start to feel like you have already received what you asked for, even before it happens. 💪🔥 🙌

Remember, no situation on earth can stop God's Word from coming true if we focus on His unchanging Word and not on the unstable world around us. 🌍🚫 God wants us to trust His Word without needing any other proof. Then, He will give us "according to our faith." 🌟🌈

When once His Word is past,
When He hath said, 'I will,' (Hebrews 13:5) 🌟
The thing shall come at last;
God keeps His promise still. (2 Corinthians 1:20) 🌈✨

The prayer of the Pentecostal age was like a check that could be cashed immediately. 💵💳 --Sir R. Anderson

"And God said… and it was so." (Genesis 1:9) 🌌🌠

Let us hold onto His promises with unwavering faith! 🌟🙏 💖
