The Key of the Winds - Streams in the Desert - May 2

"The Lord hath prepared his throne in the heavens; and his kingdom ruleth over all" (Ps. 103:19). 👑✨

One spring morning, as I left my house 🏡, a harsh east wind 💨 suddenly blew around the corner—fierce, cold, and full of dust. 😣 As I took my key 🔑 from the door, I muttered impatiently: "I wish the wind would—" I meant to say "change", but stopped mid-sentence.

As I walked 🚶‍♂️, this became a spiritual lesson. An angel 👼 appeared holding a key: "My Master sends His love and gives you this." "What is it?" I asked. "The key of the winds," he replied, then vanished.

Thrilled 😊, I rushed to the mountain caves 🏔️ where winds originate. "I'll stop this east wind from bothering us!" I locked it away 🔐 and heard echoes in the hollows. "There! No more east wind!" I said triumphantly.

"What wind should I choose instead?" 🤔 I considered the pleasant south wind 🌬️—gentle for lambs 🐑 and spring flowers 🌸. But as I inserted the key, it burned my hand! 🔥 "What am I doing?" I cried. "Who knows what damage I might cause? How do I know what the fields need?" 😰

Ashamed 😔, I prayed 🙏 for the angel to take back the key—I'd never want it again! Then the Lord Himself ✝️ appeared. He took the key, and I noticed it touched His wound marks. ❤️ I regretted complaining about anything He allows.

He hung the key on His belt. "You keep the key of the winds?" I asked. "I do, my child," He answered kindly. 😇 Looking closer, I saw all keys of my life there! 🔑

"Didn't you know My kingdom rules over all?" He asked. "Over all, Lord!" I replied. "Then... it's not safe for me to complain about anything?"

He touched me gently ✋: "Your only safety is to love, trust, and praise Me in everything." ❤️🙌

- Mark Guy Pearse
