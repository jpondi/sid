🌵 Streams in the Desert - April 6 🌅

"I will stand at my watch and station myself on the tower. I will watch to see what he will say to me."
— Habakkuk 2:1

🙏 If we don't watch for God's help, we won't receive it. Many blessings pass us by because we're not looking for them! We must be ready to open our hearts when God moves. 👀

✨ There's an old saying: "People who watch for God's work will always see it." But if you don't watch, you'll miss it! ⏳ It's like putting out water jars when it rains - if you don't prepare, you won't catch any water. 💧

💰 We need to be serious when we ask God for help. Imagine someone going to a bank, putting down a check, then picking it up without getting money - that's silly! Real customers present their checks and wait for their cash. 🏦

🔥 Many people play at praying - they don't really expect answers! But God wants us to do 🛑 REAL BUSINESS 🛑 with Him when we pray. Don't just talk about prayers - expect results! 🙌

💫 "Your hope will not be cut off." ✂️❌

— C.H. Spurgeon 📖
