🌿 God's Pattern - Streams in the Desert - April 5 🌿

"You must shut the door behind you and your sons" (2 Kings 4:4) 🔒

They needed to be alone with God ✝️. This wasn't about normal ways of doing things, human help, or even religious systems. They had to step away from everything familiar 🌌 and depend only on God 🙏, connecting directly with the source of all miracles ✨.

This is part of God's plan 📜 - a special place of being alone � with Him in prayer and faith that every fruitful soul must experience.

Sometimes God puts walls around us 🧱, removes all our usual supports, and places us in completely new situations 🆕 where only His divine power ⚡ can work. He makes us stop relying on old patterns 🧩 and trust only in Him 🤲.

Many believers live predictable lives ⏳, but those God calls to special purposes find themselves in places where they only know one thing: God is holding them 🤗 and working in ways they can't predict.

Like this widow, we must let go of outward things 👐 and hold tightly to the Lord ❤️ to see His amazing works. —Soul Food

In our hardest trials 💔, God often reveals His sweetest comforts 🍯. —Gems
