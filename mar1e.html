🌊 Streams in the Desert - March 1 🌿

📖 "Think about what God does: who can make straight what He has made crooked?" (Eccles. 7:13)

💭 God sometimes puts His people in hard situations, places them where they feel trapped 🌀, and allows things to happen that **don’t make sense** 🤯. ☁️ Yet, He is the one leading them! 🌫️

🌀 Maybe you’re in such a place right now. It feels confusing & difficult 😟—but **it is exactly where you need to be!** ✅ **In the end, you’ll see His wisdom** 🙌, and it will be a **chance for Him to show His power** ⚡.

✨ **He won’t just rescue you** 🎯—He will also **teach you something priceless** 📖 that you’ll *never forget* 🕊️. One day, through **songs & prayers** 🎶, you’ll **look back and thank Him** 💖 for doing what was best.

✍️ -- Selected

🎶 We can wait until He explains,
💡 Because we know that Jesus is in control 👑.
❓ I don’t understand, but Lord, You do.
📜 And one day, You will explain everything.

🌿 *For now, I trust it is for the best--*
🛤️ *Even the hard times have taught me to rely on You.*

🚧 *You have blocked my way, changed my plans,*
👀 *To keep my eyes fixed on You;*
💞 *To make me more humble and patient;*
⛪ *To draw my heart closer to You.*

🙏 **So I will thank You even when I don’t understand,**
🎭 *And trust You completely.*
🎉 *I know You are testing me because You love me,*
🖐️ *So I will hold on tightly to Your guiding hand.* 🤝
