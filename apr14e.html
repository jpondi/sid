Joy in the Morning - Streams in the Desert - April 14 🌅✝️

"For the Lord himself shall descend from heaven with a shout, with the voice of the archangel, and with the trump of God: and the dead in Christ shall rise first: then we which are alive and remain shall be caught up together with them in the clouds, to meet the Lord in the air: so shall we ever be with the Lord" (1 Thess. 4:16-17). 📖✨

Jesus rose from the dead very early in the morning while it was still dark. 🌄 The morning star shone on His empty tomb, not the sun. The city was still asleep when He arose. His resurrection didn't wake anyone in Jerusalem. 🌙

In the same way, Christ's Church will rise "very early in the morning while it is still dark," with only the morning star shining. ✨ Like Jesus, believers will awake while others are still asleep in spiritual darkness. Their rising won't disturb anyone. The world won't hear God calling them home. 🏠

Just as Jesus gently laid His saints to rest in their graves, He will gently wake them when the time comes. 💐 They will hear the life-giving words: "Awake and sing, you who live in the dust" (Isa. 26:19). The first rays of glory will shine into their tombs. ☀️

They will experience the first signs of morning - its fresh smell, quiet peace, clean air, and hopeful solitude. All these wonderful things will belong to them. 🌿

What a difference between these joys and the dark night they've come through! What a change from the grave they're leaving behind! ⚡ As they throw off their earthly bodies and rise with new heavenly bodies to meet Jesus in the sky, the Morning Star will guide them - just like the Bethlehem Star once guided others to the newborn King. 🌟

"Weeping may last for a night, but joy comes in the morning." 😢➡️😊

"While crowds shout 'Hosanna,' Jesus will come down from heaven with His glorified saints and attending angels. With grace shining like a halo, He will welcome His own." 👑

"Yes, come quickly, Lord Jesus!" 🙏

A Christian soldier once said: "When I die, don't play taps at my grave. Play reveille instead - the morning call, the music that says 'Wake up!'" 🎺

-- Horatius Bonar ✍️
