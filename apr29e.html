Streams in the Desert - April 29

"Elias was a man subject to like passions as we are" (James 5:17). 🙏

Thank God for that! 🌈 Elijah hid under a juniper tree, just like we sometimes hide from problems. 😔 He complained and doubted, just like we often do. But everything changed when he truly connected with God. ✨

🔹 Even though Elijah was "a man like us," the Bible says "he prayed praying."

👉 This means he didn't just pray once - he kept on praying! 🙏⏳

Climb up Mount Carmel with me 🏔️ and see this amazing lesson about Faith vs Sight. Elijah didn't need fire from heaven now - he needed rain! ☔ The same man who called down fire could call down rain using the same method: persistent prayer.

"He bowed to the ground with his face between his knees" - this means he blocked out all distractions. 🙈

He told his servant: "Go look toward the sea." The servant returned and said just one word: "Nothing!" 🌊

👉 What would we do in this situation? 🤔

We might say: "I knew this would happen!" and stop praying. ❌ But Elijah? He said: "Go again." 🔄 The servant returned: "Nothing!" Elijah: "Go again." "Nothing!"

Finally, the servant returned saying: "I see a small cloud, as small as a man's hand." ☁️ Soon, heavy rain fell! 🌧️ King Ahab couldn't even race back to Samaria fast enough with his chariot! 🐎

🔺 This story shows the difference between Faith and Sight:

👉 Faith stays focused on God 🙏
👉 Sight sees only problems 👀
👉 Faith keeps praying even when things look hopeless ⏳

Do you know how to pray like this? 💪 How to pray until something happens? Even when everything looks bad, don't focus on that. Your living God is still in heaven! 🌟 Sometimes delays are part of His perfect plan. ⏳❤️

🔺 Three boys explained what faith means:
  1. The first said: "Taking hold of Christ" 👦✝️
  2. The second: "Keeping hold" 👧🤲
  3. The third: "Not letting go" 👶💪
God's blessings be with you! ✝️🙌
