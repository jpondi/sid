🌅 Streams in the Desert - March 2

🕊️ "Be ready in the morning, and come... present thyself there to me in the top of the mount. And no man shall come up with thee." (Exod. 34:2-3)

🌞✨ Starting your day with God is important. You should not begin your day 🌅 without spending time with God 🙏, and you should not meet others 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 before you have met with Him 👀. You cannot expect to win 🏆🎯 if you start your day with only your own strength 💪.

🙏 Before you begin your work 📅, take a few quiet moments 🤫 with your heart ❤️ and God. Do not meet others 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦, even your family 🏡, until you have first spent time with the great Guest 🎖️ and honored Friend 🤍 of your life—Jesus Christ ✝️.

  • 🤍 Meet Him alone 🤲.
  • 📅 Meet Him every day ⏳.
  • 📖 Read His Book 📜 of wisdom and go through your daily tasks ✅❌ knowing that He is guiding you in everything you do 🎯.

🌄 Start the day with God! 🙌

☀️ He is your Sun 🌞 and Light!

🌅 He brings brightness ✨ to your morning;

🎶 Sing to Him 🎼 with joy.

🎵 Sing a new song in the morning! 🎤🎶

🌳 Sing with the trees 🌲 and hills 🏔️;

💨 Sing with the winds 🍃, the seas 🌊, and the fields 🌾,

🌸 Sing with the flowers 🌻 and rivers 💦.

🎤 Sing your first song to God! 🎼🎶

🚫 Not to people 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦;

🙌 Not to the things He made 🐦,

✨ But to the glorious One 🌟.

🚶‍♂️ Take your first walk with God! 🛤️

👣 Let Him walk with you 🏃‍♂️;

🏞️ By a stream 💧, or the sea 🌊, or a mountain path 🏔️,

🤲 Stay close to Him 🤍.

💼 Your first work of the day 💰

☝️ Should be with God ☁️;

📈 Then your work will go well 📊,

❤️ And your day will be filled with love 💖.

— ✍️ Horatius Bonar
