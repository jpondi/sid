When God Opens Our Eyes 👀🔥

"Then Elisha prayed, 'O Lord, open his eyes so he can see.' The Lord opened the servant's eyes and he saw that the hill was full of horses and chariots of fire all around Elisha." (2 Kings 6:17) 🐎🔥

This is the prayer we all need - for ourselves and for each other: "Lord, open our eyes so we can see!" 👀🙏 Because just like in Elisha's time, our world is full of God's protection and power - heavenly chariots ready to carry us to victory! 🏆✨

When God opens our eyes, we begin to see everything differently:
🌞 Happy moments become chariots of joy
🌧️ Hard times become chariots of growth
🙏 Ordinary days become chariots of purpose

"Everything that happens can become a chariot for your soul if you let it." 🚗💨

The choice is ours 🤔💭:
🔴 We can let problems crush us like heavy carts (Juggernauts) 😫
🟢 OR we can climb into them like victory chariots 🚀

It's not about what happens to us, but about how we respond:
💪 Stand up = Chariot to victory
😵 Lie down = Gets crushed

"The Lord cannot do much with a crushed soul." 💔😔

Satan tries to make us feel hopeless 😈 about:
• Our personal struggles
• Problems in church
• World situations

Just like an army with low spirits will lose battles ⚔️, or how a missionary got sick when her spirit became weak 😷, we must guard our hearts ❤️🛡️.

Satan's game plan 📜:
1. First knock us off balance 🤸‍♂️
2. Then wear us out with long struggles ⏳
3. Finally make us give up 🏳️

But remember! God's chariots of fire are always surrounding you 🔥🐎. Don't stop declaring victory 🗣️🏆!
