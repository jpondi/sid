God Cannot Lie - Streams in the Desert - May 1

"God that cannot lie promised" (Titus 1:2). ✨

Faith isn't about forcing yourself to believe something will happen. 💪
It's about seeing as fact that God has said it will happen, knowing it's true, and resting in that truth. 🕊️

🔹 Faith turns God's promise into a prophecy. 📜➡️🔮
A promise needs our cooperation, but when faith claims it, it becomes certain - because God cannot lie! ✝️
- Days of Heaven upon Earth

Many pray for more faith, but often they really want to see proof rather than to believe. 👀
True faith doesn't say: "I see this is good, so God must have sent it."
Instead it says: "God sent this, so it must be good for me." 🙌

- Phillips Brooks
The Shepherd doesn't ask for faith in your faith,
But simply faith in Him alone; ✝️
In light or dark, just do His will,
Let Jesus perfect what He's begun. 🌟
