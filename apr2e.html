🌄 Streams in the Desert - April 2 🌄

✨ "They looked... and behold, the glory of the Lord appeared in the cloud" (Exodus 16:10) ✨

🌥️ Train yourself to look for the bright side of every dark cloud—and when you find it, keep looking at it, not at the gloomy gray center.

💪 Never give in to discouragement, no matter how hard life gets. A discouraged person is helpless—they can't fight life's battles or pray effectively for others. 🛡️

🐍 Run from discouragement like you'd run from a poisonous snake! Turn away quickly, unless you want to lie defeated in the dust. 🏃‍♂️

📖 Search for God's promises and declare out loud: "This promise is mine!" If doubt still creeps in, tell God everything and ask Him to silence the enemy attacking your mind. 🙏

⚡ The moment you reject doubt and discouragement, the Holy Spirit will strengthen your faith and fill you with God's power. At first you might not feel it, but as you keep refusing negative thoughts, you'll sense the darkness retreating! ✨

👀 If only we could see the army of angels supporting us when we turn from darkness to God—we'd never fear the enemy's attempts to discourage us! 😇

🛡️ All of God's mighty power stands with even the weakest believer who trusts in Christ and turns to Him for help. —Selected

🦅 Story of the Eagle: One autumn day, I saw a prairie eagle shot by a hunter. Even dying, his eyes still shone bright. He slowly turned his head for one last longing look at the sky—his true home where he once flew with the winds and lightnings. Now he lay dying because he flew too low just once. Our souls are like that eagle. Earth isn't our home—we must keep looking heavenward! Keep faith, hope, courage, and Christ. If we won't be brave, we should leave life's battlefield now. There's no time to panic. Keep looking up, my soul! 🌤️

🌟 Keep looking up—

🌟 Keep looking up—

Though darkness covers your soul;

God's light will make you whole

When you look up. 💡

🌟 Keep looking up—

When tired from the fight;

When you look up. ⚔️
