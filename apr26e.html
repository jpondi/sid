✨ Streams in the Desert - April 26 🌵

"I regard all things as worthless compared to the surpassing value of knowing Christ Jesus my Lord... I consider them garbage, that I may gain Christ" ✝️ (Phil 3:8)

💡 Shining always costs something. Light only comes when something burns. A candle that's not lit gives no light. 🔥 Burning must happen before shining. We can't truly help others without it costing us something. Burning means suffering - and we naturally avoid pain.

We often think we're most useful when we're strong and active - when our hands are full of good works. 🤲

But when we're set aside to just suffer... when we're sick... in constant pain... when all our activities stop... we feel useless. 😔 Like we're not doing anything important.

Yet if we're patient and surrendered, we may bless the world more through our suffering than through all our busy service. We're burning now - and shining because we're burning.

🌱 "The glory of tomorrow is rooted in today's hard work."

👑 Many want the crown without the cross,
✨ The shining without the burning,
✝️ But crucifixion comes before coronation.

🌸 The Aloe Plant's Tale
☀️ In sunny lands it grows so slow,
⏳ A hundred years to bloom and show;
💐 Then bursts with flowers - a glorious sight,
🌺 The pride of tropics, pure and bright.
🌱 But the plant must die to give this flower,
✨ One glorious bloom - its final hour.

🌿 And yet from each dropped blossom fair,
🌱 New plants spring up to grow right there;
🔄 A thousand lives from one death true,
🌍 The old makes way for life anew.

🦅 The Pelican's Tale
🏜️ In lonely desert lands it flies,
💧 Bringing water through the skies;
🐟 Fishing seas to feed its young,
❤️ Greatest love e'er to be sung.
🩸 In famine gives its very blood,
✝️ Dies to feed them - love so good.

✝️ The Greatest Tale of All
🌍 He died - yet lives in souls untold,
✨ His life still changing young and old;
🌱 His seed now fills the earth below,
🌟 As stars above in heaven glow.
❤️ He taught to lose our life with cheer,
☮️ That love's true life might then appear.
✝️ His death our life, His loss our gain,
😊 Joy for tears, peace for pain.

—Selected
