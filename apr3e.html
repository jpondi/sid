🔥 Streams in the Desert - April 3 🔥

✨ "Glorify ye the Lord in the fires" (Isaiah 24:15). ✨

Notice the small but powerful word "in"! 🙌 We are to honor God in our trials—right in the middle of what truly hurts. 🔥 While sometimes God protects His people from feeling the pain, usually fire burns.

But this is exactly where we glorify Him—by showing complete trust in His goodness and love, even when hard things happen to us. 💖 And more than that, we believe that something greater will come from this trial—something that brings Him more praise than if we hadn't gone through it. 🌱➡️🌻

Some fires are so hot that only strong faith can walk through them—weak faith will fail. 🚶‍♂️🔥 We must win the victory while still in the furnace. 🏆

🦁 "A person shows their true faith during hard times. The men thrown into the fiery furnace came out exactly as they went in—except their chains were gone!" ⛓️➡️✨

How often God uses our fires of trouble to burn away what binds us! 🔥 Those three men weren't hurt at all—no burns, no singed hair, not even the smell of smoke on their clothes. 👕 That's how Christians should come through trials—free from what held them down, but untouched by the flames. ✝️

🌟 "Triumphing over them in it" (Colossians 2:15). 🌟

That's real victory—winning while still in the sickness, while still facing death, while still surrounded by problems. 💪 Oh, believe this: there's a power that makes us more than conquerors! There are heights where we can look back at our journey and sing victory songs even before reaching Heaven. 🎶 We can seem rich when poor, and make others rich through our poverty. Our triumph comes while still in the battle. Christ's greatest triumph came in His suffering—maybe ours does too. ✝️

💖 "Isn't it amazing to see someone weighed down by troubles yet keeping a joyful heart? Or someone terribly tempted but still winning? Or a suffering traveler whose patience never breaks? This shows God's power at work!" 🌈

🌊 When all supports crumble away,
And life feels like a stormy sea,
Can you remain—through darkest day—
A miracle of peace, set free? 🕊️
