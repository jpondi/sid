Streams in the Desert - March 15

"Don’t be afraid, despised insignificant Jacob, men of Israel. I am helping you," says the Lord, your protector, the Holy One of Israel. "Look, I am making you like a sharp threshing sledge, new and double-edged. You will thresh the mountains and crush them; you will make the hills like straw." (Isaiah 41:14-15) 🌄🔥

Could any two things be more different than a worm and a sharp tool with teeth? 🐛⚒️ The worm is fragile—it can be crushed by a stone or a wheel. But a tool with teeth is strong; it can break rocks and leave its mark. 💪✨

Our mighty God can change the weak into the strong. He can take a person or a nation, as helpless as a worm, and fill them with His Spirit’s power. 🌟 This strength can leave a lasting impact on history. 📜

So, if you feel like a worm, take heart! 💖 God can make you stronger than your struggles. He can turn everything around for your good. 🌈 With His strength, even disappointments can become opportunities for growth. 💎 When God gives us strong wills, we can overcome any challenge, just like an iron tool cuts through tough soil. ⚔️🌱

"I will make thee," and He will do it! 🙌

—Dr. Jowett

Christ is building His kingdom with broken things. 🏰💔 People often want only the strong, successful, and unbroken to build their kingdoms. But God loves the weak, the failed, and the broken. 🌟 Heaven is filled with lives that were once shattered, but Christ has restored them to beauty and joy. 🌈✨ He can take a life crushed by pain and turn it into a harp that sings praises. 🎶 He can lift even the saddest failure to heavenly glory. 🌠

—J. R. Miller
