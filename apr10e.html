🌿 Developing Grace - Streams in the Desert - April 10 🌿

"Show me why you are fighting with me" (Job 10:2).

✨ Dear struggling friend, maybe God is allowing this hard time to help your good qualities grow. Some of your best qualities would never be seen without difficulties! ✨

🌦️ Your faith doesn't shine as bright in easy times as it does in hard times. Love is like a glowworm - it shows its light best in darkness. Hope is like a star - you can't see it during the day, only at night! 💫

💎 Hard times are like the dark velvet that makes diamonds sparkle. God uses struggles to make your good qualities shine brighter! 💎

🙏 Remember when you prayed: "Lord, I'm afraid I don't have enough faith - please show me that I do"? Without realizing it, you were actually asking for trials! How else can you know you have faith unless it gets tested? 🧗‍♂️

⚔️ God often sends challenges so:
1️⃣ We can discover our strengths
2️⃣ We can be sure they're really there
3️⃣ We can grow even stronger! ⚔️

🎖️ God doesn't train His soldiers by letting them relax in comfortable tents. He makes them:
- 🏞️ Cross dangerous rivers
- ⛰️ Climb steep mountains
- 🚶 Walk long, tiring miles with heavy packs 🎖️

💭 So Christian, could this be why you're facing troubles now? Could this be why God is allowing this struggle in your life? 💭

- C.H. Spurgeon 🌟
